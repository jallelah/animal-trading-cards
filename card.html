Jellyfish



They've been around for more than 650 million years, and there are thousands of different species, with more species discovered all of the time.

  • Scientific Name: Medusozoa
  • Family Name: Chirodropidae
  • Life Span: One year
  • Diet: Carnivore

Jellyfish have tiny stinging cells in their tentacles to stun or paralyze their prey before they eat them. Inside their bell-shaped body is an opening that is its mouth. They eat and discard waste from this opening.
